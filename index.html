I am a PhD student in the Physics department of Syracuse University. I work in J.M.Schwarz Theory Group. I have worked on the problem of compression stiffening of a single cell under uniaxial strain, nonlinearities in a multi-phase morphogenesis model of the cerebellum, the correspondence between convexity and rigidity for spring networks under isotropic strain and using statistical physics to make maximally random null models for mice neural networks. I also work on constructing a spin model for studying the first order transition in the T4 bacteriophage tail-sheath.
